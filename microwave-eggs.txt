Ingredients
- 2 eggs
- tablespoon of shredded cheese
- tablespoon each of onions, tomatoes, or other veg

Directions:
1. Coat a microwavable bowl or very large mug with a non-stick coating
2. Break the eggs into the bowl
3. Add cheese and veggies
4. Stir with a fork to break the egg yolks and mix everything
5. Microwave on high for 45 seconds
6. Take out the bowl and stir again
7. Microwave on high for another 45 seconds
